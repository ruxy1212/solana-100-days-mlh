Transfer Fee: This extension automatically deducts a small percentage from every transaction and holds it in the recipient's account until the token creator withdraws it.

Interest-Bearing: This extension causes the displayed balance of a token to grow over time based on a set rate, rewarding holders without needing to send them new tokens manually.

The transfer fee extension used in Day50 deducts a percentage from each transfer and then allows the issuer to withdraw the accumulated fees, while the interest-bearing extension from Day52 automatically increases token balances over time based on a configured interest rate.
